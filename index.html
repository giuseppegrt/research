About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Management, conducting research in the field of Cognition and Perception of Data Visualizations, and teaching the practical component of the Econometrics course at the University of Neuchâtel. I completed my undergraduate studies in Italy and Switzerland, specializing in Economics, and participated in several international programs, projects, and conferences, holding roles in different organizations. Fluent in four languages, my experiences enable me to have a broad skill set in both scientific and managerial fields.
